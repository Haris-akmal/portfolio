European Pharmacy Sales & Profitability Intelligence Dashboard

Tools Used

Power BI, Power Query, Excel

Data Source

European Pharmacy Chain Sales Dataset (ZoomCharts Data Challenge)

Background

Pharmacy distributors operate across multiple European countries, generating large volumes of daily transactional sales data. However, without structured analysis, it becomes difficult to understand regional performance, product profitability, and operational efficiency. This project transforms pharmacy sales data into an interactive Power BI dashboard that enables stakeholders to analyze revenue, margin, geographic contribution, and product performance through intuitive drill-down exploration.

Objective

  • Monitor overall revenue, margin, and sales volume trends
  • Identify top and underperforming countries, regions, and pharmacies
  • Analyze Urban, Suburban, and Rural pharmacy performance differences
  • Evaluate product category and brand contribution
  • Assess the impact of promotions on revenue and profitability
  • Support data-driven decisions to improve profitability and regional growth

Data Preparation

Data cleaning and transformation were performed using Power Query to ensure reliable and accurate analysis:

  • Removed invalid sales records (negative revenue, cost, or zero units sold)
  • Standardized promotion flag values (YES / NO)
  • Verified revenue and cost calculations for margin consistency
  • Ensured correct data types for date and numeric fields
  • Prepared hierarchical fields for drill-down analysis

Data Modeling

A structured star schema model was implemented in Power BI to support scalable and efficient analysis:

  • Central fact table containing daily pharmacy sales transactions
  • Date dimension for time-based and seasonal analysis
  • Pharmacy dimension including country, region, and pharmacy type
  • Product dimension structured by category, brand, and product
  • DAX measures created for consistent KPI calculations

Key Metrics (DAX-Driven KPIs)

  • Total Revenue
  • Total Margin
  • Margin %
  • Total Units Sold
  • Promo vs Non-Promo Revenue
  • Pharmacy Revenue Ranking

Project Overview

Key Analytical Insights

  • Certain countries and regions contribute disproportionately to overall revenue and margin
  • Performance varies significantly across pharmacies within the same region
  • Urban pharmacies generally generate higher sales volumes
  • Some high-volume products deliver relatively low margins
  • Promotions increase sales volume but may reduce overall margin percentage
  • Clear seasonal sales patterns support better inventory planning

Final Thoughts

This analysis demonstrates that the pharmacy business is operating profitably, with a stable average profit margin and strong overall sales performance. However, performance varies across branches and promotional strategies.

  • Branch Performance: Some branches generate high revenue, while others achieve higher efficiency through stronger profit margins.
  • Promotion Impact: Promotions successfully increase sales volume but reduce profit margin per unit.
  • Profitability Focus: Sustainable growth depends not only on increasing sales but also on maintaining healthy margins.
  • Strategic Insight: Data-driven decisions can help optimize pricing, promotion timing, and branch performance.

Overall, this dashboard enables management to move beyond raw sales numbers and focus on balanced growth — maximizing both revenue and profitability through informed strategic decisions.
